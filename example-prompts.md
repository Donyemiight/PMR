# Example Prompts

Realistic natural-language prompts that should trigger PMR. Use these as test cases.

## Discovery

> "What prediction markets are live on Pharos testnet right now?"

> "List crypto-price markets with the highest 24h volume."

> "Show me all markets resolving in the next 7 days."

## Query

> "What's the current YES price on `0xabc…123`?"

> "Give me the live state of the BTC-100k market."

> "How much liquidity is in market `0xdef…456`?"

## Positions

> "Show my positions in `0xabc…123`."

> "What markets does `0x123…abc` have shares in, and what's the current value?"

> "How much can I claim from resolved markets?"

## Analysis

> "Analyze the BTC-100k market — show me the 24h trend and holder concentration."

> "Is the liquidity in `0xabc…123` healthy?"

> "What's the volatility on the ETH-5k market over the last week?"

## Report

> "Write me a full research report on `0xabc…123`."

> "Generate a markdown report for the top-volume market in the pharos-ecosystem category."

> "Save a report for the FED-rate-decision market to my current directory."

## Execute

> "Buy $50 of YES on `0xabc…123` with 2% slippage."

> "Sell all my NO shares in `0xdef…456`."

> "Claim my winnings from the resolved market `0x789…012`."

> "I want to put 100 PHRS into the YES outcome of the next Pharos mainnet launch market."
